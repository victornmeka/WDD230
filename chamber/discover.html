Discover Eko(Lagos/Lasgidi)

Welcome to Eko,the ("Centre of Excellence"). "Eko" officially known as "Lagos" is often described as the cultural, financial, and entertainment Capital of Africa. In Africa today Eko inarguably plays a vital role in commerce, fashion,tourism, art, technology , and entertainment.

The freedom park was fomerly a prison that hosted some political activists who fought for Nigeria's independence. It was re-built in October, 2010 to commemorate the 50th anniversary independence celebration.

The National art Theatre is the primary centre for art perfomance in Nigeria.

The Lagos National Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium.

Lekki Conservation Centre in Lagos, Nigeria is a nature reserve, one of the most visited tourist attractions in Nigeria. It has a 401-metre long canopy walk which is longest canopy walk in Africa.

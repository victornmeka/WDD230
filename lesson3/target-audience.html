Target Audience

Target audiences includes entreprenuers, small businesses looking to grow their businesses within Lagos state, big corporations looking to patner with small local businesses. Tourists are also welcome to experience our local market/businesses giving them(the tourrists) the experience of tourism and our local businesses the exposure it needs. The Eko Chambers of Commerce will be working with the local and state government officials to ensure that major state projects are handled by these local business owners and professionals.
